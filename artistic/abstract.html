To me, the abstract is a total freedom of expression, to create something that is different and unexpected! Sometimes it's a way to have fun exploring with media and colors, sometimes they are created as an expression from my heart. Each one is different, and the interpretations of each one is solely dependent on the eyes and vision of the viewer.
