The motivation for this series began when I was moved to create Lonely Nights at the end of 2004. It was not until early 2006 that these canvases just burst forth. All of these paintings are created "impasto" style and each one was created from an outpouring of emotions from inside my soul.

When the muse strikes, there is no help for it but to paint.

For information or questions about works for sale or commissions, please send me an email!
