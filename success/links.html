This is a collection of some of my favorite links, some are thought provoking, some are a lot of fun, some are enjoyable - but all of them mean something to me, and maybe to you as well!

 
 

Daily Om - This page has some wonderful introspective thoughts, you can get on the mailing list for this!

Quado - I love Carrie's thoughts

The Great Illusion - A wonderful epic poem - at least 35 pages long - complete with images of Jim Warren's art (also pretty amazing to see, realism mixed with surrealism) This poem speaks of what "reality" really is. It's pretty mind bending! (disclaimer, leave yourself lots of time to read it all)

The Invitation - by Oriah Mountain Dreamer

The Inspiration Peak - A great site for inspirational gems ranging from quotes to stories to poems and more! They had a mailing list as well for receiving thoughts/quotes on a periodic basis.

The Awakening - A sort of epiphany after those dark times...

The Book of Now - All we do have is this moment in time... and this is a fun 'book' to turn the pages on!
